DID YOU KNOW?

The biggest fish ever caught on record is a 2,664 lbs Great White Shark that was caught in 1959 by Alfred Dean of Irymple, Victoria. He managed to do this with a porpoise as bait on a 130-pound line, all within a matter of 50 minutes!
